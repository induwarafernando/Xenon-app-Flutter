# Xenon Fashion App

Welcome to Xenon Fashion App, where style meets convenience! This Flutter-powered mobile application is your gateway to a world of fashion, offering a seamless shopping experience right at your fingertips. With its sleek design and robust features, Xenon Fashion App redefines how you shop for your favorite clothing and accessories.

## Features

- **Welcome Page**: Get greeted with style as you step into the Xenon Fashion App world.
- **Login Page**: Securely access your account with ease using our intuitive login interface.
- **Registration Page**: Join the fashion revolution by creating your account hassle-free.
- **Shop Now Page**: Dive into curated collections and discover the latest trends effortlessly.
- **Home Page**: Experience personalized recommendations and stay up-to-date with rotating carousels of must-have items.
- **Add to Cart Feature**: Shopping made simple - add your desired items to the cart with just a tap.
- **Order Live Tracking**: Keep tabs on your orders in real-time with our GPS-powered live tracking feature.
- **Order List Page**: Access your order history and manage your purchases conveniently.

## Installation

1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/yourusername/xenon-fashion-app.git`
2. Navigate to the project directory: `cd xenon-fashion-app`
3. Run the app: `flutter run`

## Usage

1. Dive into the world of fashion by exploring the welcoming, login, and registration pages.
2. Discover new collections and featured items on the Shop Now page.
3. Easily add your favorite items to the cart with the Add to Cart feature.
4. Stay in control of your orders with real-time tracking on the Order Live Tracking page.
5. Manage your past and current orders effortlessly on the Order List page.
